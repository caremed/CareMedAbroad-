Blog: Is MBBS Abroad Safe for Indian Students?

MBBS abroad has gained popularity due to rising costs in India. Countries like Kazakhstan, Russia, and Georgia offer affordable education. But is it safe?

Yes, if you choose WHO/NMC-approved colleges and avoid agents. Kazakhstan, especially Almaty, offers Indian food, hostels, and English-medium classes. Caspian ISM is a top choice among Indian students.

Parents should verify admission documents, fee structure, and FMGE pass rates. Students must adapt to cultural shifts and climate. Safety is excellent in university zones. Overall, MBBS abroad is a safe and smart move if guided correctly.
